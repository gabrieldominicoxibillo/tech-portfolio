About Me

I'm an artist with a passion for programming. Over the past two years, I have dedicated 11-13 hours a day to honing my skills. After a few months, I transitioned to studying robotics, which I found to be the most effective way to explore emerging technologies. Beginning with Arduino, I later expanded my expertise to include Raspberry Pi. In my spare time, I accept commissions for customizing Wi-Fi portal designs for authentication purposes. As my understanding of software-hardware interactions deepened, I proposed and defended our robotics thesis/capstone. This project utilized RFID cards as access keys/credit cards, with data sourced from the Raspberry Pi acting as a server. The Raspberry Pi sends signals to the Arduino to activate its actuators for dispensation. Additionally, a computer screen was integrated to allow administrators to monitor machine activities, with email notifications triggered upon each transaction completion. I was even recognized in our thesis defense by our electronics engineering teacher in front of 10 panelists for being outstanding in the field of robotics.     The dashboard view offers administrators comprehensive administration control, providing centralized monitoring of transactions, management of customer details, adjustment of product prices and stocks, and machine status monitoring. Additional pages, such as the products view, transaction history page, customers page, and customer details page, provided a comprehensive overview and management of available products, account transactions, registered users, and individual account information

  • Dashboard View

    This showcases the login view of the system, specifically designed for administrators to access the dashboard. This interface serves as the initial point of entry, requiring authorized credentials for authentication.

  • Customers' Page

    This webpage depicts the customers page, an essential component of the system that provides the overview of all registered users. This interface displays key information, including the unique user identifier (UID), names, emails, and balances associated with each registered account.

  • Account Details

    This webpage showcases the customer details page, a feature of the system that enables customers connected to the same network via a router to access and view their account information. This interface provides customers with essential details, including their remaining balance, name, last transaction date, and email address.

  • Product Selection

    This presents the products view, a page in the system that displays all available products along with their corresponding prices and stock levels. This interface serves as a comprehensive resource for administrators to both view and edit product details.
